Services

Modular or end-to-end support, from single-target docking to large-scale screening and complex trajectory analysis.

Service 01

Molecular Docking

Structure-based virtual screening and pose prediction using state-of-the-art docking engines and carefully validated workflows.

  • Protein & ligand preparation and quality checks
  • Binding site definition and grid generation
  • Docking, scoring, and hit ranking
  • Interaction analysis & 2D/3D visualization
Ideal for: hit identification, lead optimization, and hypothesis-driven design.
Service 02

Molecular Dynamics (MD) Simulation

Time-resolved simulations to evaluate complex stability, binding mode robustness, and key structural changes.

  • System building (solvent, ions, force-field selection)
  • Energy minimization, equilibration, production runs
  • RMSD, RMSF, Rg, H-bond, SASA analysis
  • Free-energy-related metrics (e.g., MM/PBSA, MM/GBSA)
Ideal for: mechanism insights, stability assessments, and supporting publications.
Service 03

Data Analysis & Reporting

Clean, interpretable outputs tailored for manuscripts, theses, grant applications, and internal R&D reports.

  • Custom plots and visualizations for docking/MD results
  • Statistical analysis and figure standardization
  • Summary slides and technical reports
  • Guidance for methods and results sections
Deliverables ready for direct inclusion in publications and presentations.
